Code of Conduct

We require that every participant in an online conference represent themselves by the name that they use for their professional work. In addition, any speech or imagery that are posted in the chats of the various conference activities must be appropriate.

Participants who violate this code may be sanctioned and/or expelled from the event, at the discretion of the Symposium Chairs. If you experience harassment or discriminatory behavior during the symposium, we encourage you to reach out to Symposium Chairs (email below).
